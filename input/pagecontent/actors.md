### Actors
 
The following ActorDefinitions define the systems that plays a role in data exchange, and that have obligations associated with the role the actor plays, in Health Connect.

- [Health Connect Reader Actor](ActorDefinition-reader-actor-health-connect.html)

The Health Connect Reader is a system that creates and initiates a data access request to retrieve core digital health and administrative information. The set of capabilities (behaviours) for this actor is defined in [Health Connect Reader CapabilityStatement](CapabilityStatement-health-connect-reader.html).
